Labs continue to research and synthesize meat from algae, fungi, and microorganisms found on plant roots. We have partnered with Improbable Beef, a food startup to research candidate species for the elusive bacteria that will provide the perfect taste. The next best taste lies within the human body. The human body is a source of thousands of types of bacteria, and different parts of the body harbor different species. The ideal bacterial species to make synthetic beef may be found in the belly button. The data below show the findings of bacterial species that colonize in our belly buttons.
